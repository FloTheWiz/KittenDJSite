Kitten DJs

Dashnet Forum's Music Bot

Kitten DJs is a WIP music bot for Dashnet Forums.

All commands below work as slash commands /, but also work with the bot's prefix ; or by pinging the bot @Kitten DJs #2957.

Command Help

<Parameter> is required

[Parameter] is optional

| refers to mutually exclusive arguments

Music

Play music in the Music Channel on Dashnet Forums.

Join

/join [Channel]

Joins the voice channel you're in, or a specified one.

Aliases

;j ;summon

Play

/play <Song Title | Youtube Link>

Plays a song or adds it to the queue. Also summons the bot if it's not already in your channel.

Aliases

;p ;add ;append ;a ;blast

Now Playing

/nowplaying

Shows the currently playing song.

Aliases

;np ;playing ;current

Queue

/queue [page number]

Shows up to 25 songs. Use page number for if your queue has more than 1 page.

Aliases

;q ;list ;l

Remove

/remove <index>

Removes the song at the specified index. Use -1 for the last queued song.

Aliases

;del ;r

Skip

/skip

Votes to skip the current song.

Aliases

;s
